Title: "A simple rhythm for showing up well"
Description: "Start with the bottle and the audience, then shape the right mix of education, appearances, retail support, and follow-up."

Four steps:
1. Learn the brand, the bottle, and the audience you want to reach.
2. Shape the right mix of tastings, account visits, education, and event support.
3. Represent the brand with a consistent voice in the room.
4. Follow up with the signals, questions, and opportunities worth acting on.
